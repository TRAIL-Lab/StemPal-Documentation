### AI for Learning in STEM ✨

StemPal is a research-focused suite of AI-assisted browser extensions developed by a Lehigh University education research team. The suite currently includes:

- `MathPal` : support for mathematics learning
- `StatPal` : support for statistics learning
- `iCodePal` : support for block-based coding learning
- `HackPal` : support for computer science learning

Welcome to visit the [official StemPal website](https://home.stempal.us).

## Purpose 🎯

StemPal is designed for educational research, guided classroom pilots, and nonprofit academic collaboration. This repository is intended to provide public-facing documentation for the broader StemPal package and its deployment context.

## Research and Access Notice 🔒

StemPal is not released as a general-purpose commercial product.

- The StemPal package and its extensions are provided for research and scholarly use only.
- StemPal is part of a research effort whose data practices and school collaboration model have been reviewed through Lehigh University IRB.
- StemPal is a nonprofit educational research product developed for supervised educational use and institutional collaboration.
- Access is provided through direct collaboration with schools, educational institutions, and research partners who contact the team directly.
- Commercial deployment, resale, sublicensing, or unsupervised redistribution is not authorized.


## Team 🎓
